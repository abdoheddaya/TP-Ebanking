DIGITAL BANKING FAQ

Q: What types of accounts are available?
A: We offer two types of accounts: Current Accounts and Saving Accounts.
Current Accounts come with an overdraft facility.
Saving Accounts earn interest at a competitive rate.

Q: What is an overdraft?
A: An overdraft allows you to withdraw more money than you currently 
have in your Current Account, up to a pre-approved limit of 9000 MAD.

Q: What is the interest rate on saving accounts?
A: Our saving accounts currently offer an interest rate of 5.5% per year.

Q: What operations can I perform on my account?
A: You can perform three types of operations:
- Credit: deposit money into your account
- Debit: withdraw money from your account
- Transfer: move money between two accounts

Q: What happens if I have insufficient balance for a debit?
A: If your account balance is insufficient to cover the requested 
debit amount, the operation will be rejected with an error message.

Q: What are the account statuses?
A: An account can have one of three statuses:
- CREATED: account has just been opened
- ACTIVATED: account is active and operational
- SUSPENDED: account has been temporarily suspended

Q: How can I check my account history?
A: You can view your paginated transaction history by providing 
your account ID to the chatbot or through the web application.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can reach our support team at support@digitalbank.ma
or visit any of our branches during business hours 9am-5pm.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. All communications are secured with JWT authentication 
and all sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Q: What currencies are supported?
A: Currently we support MAD (Moroccan Dirham) for all operations.
